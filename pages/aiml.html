About Department

The Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning department focuses on intelligent systems, data-driven decision making, sensor networks, and computational modelling for advanced engineering solutions.

The academic curriculum covers machine learning, deep learning, neural networks, computer vision, natural language processing, data mining, and AI ethics. It bridges the gap between core computer science and advanced data analysis.

In addition to the basic degree course, the department runs specialized Honors pathways in emerging fields to meet the massive global demand for AI engineers and data professionals.

Our students participate in national-level technical competitions like the Smart India Hackathon (SIH), coding contests, and research paper presentations, showcasing their innovation in intelligent technologies.

Graduates are ready for career roles such as AI engineer, data scientist, machine learning model developer, and analyst at leading technology firms and startups, with robust placement support from the institute.

We collaborate with industry partners, research centers, and government innovation initiatives to give students hands-on exposure and real-world project experience under expert guidance.
